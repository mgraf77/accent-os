# MOBILE COMMAND CENTER MODEL — ACCENTOS
> **Focus:** High-Frequency Operational Triage

The mobile experience of AccentOS is not a "shrunk-down desktop." It is a dedicated triage surface designed for use "in the aisle" or "between meetings."

---

## 1. THE OPERATIONAL RAIL
The "Status Bar" for the business, persistent at the top or bottom of the mobile viewport.

- **Pulse Dots:** 6-8 micro-indicators for core health dimensions (Inventory, Sales, Fulfillment, eCommerce, etc.).
- **Momentum Indicator:** A simple up/down arrow next to the Pulse showing 24h direction.
- **Urgent Count:** A badge indicating high-severity signals needing immediate triage.

---

## 2. EXECUTIVE SCAN FLOWS
Designed for the "30-second check-in."

- **Vertical Signal Feed:** High-density cards, one signal per card.
- **Severity Filtering:** Toggle between "All" and "Urgent Only" with a single thumb-tap.
- **Visual Hierarchy:** Large "Severity Icon" (Red/Yellow) + Bold Title + Primary Metric.

---

## 3. QUICK ACTIONS (The "Thumb" Menu)
The FAB (Floating Action Button) is the primary engine of mobile productivity.

- **Context-Aware:** Changes based on the active signal or page.
- **Primary Actions:**
  - `+ Quote` (Voice-to-note enabled)
  - `✓ Resolve Signal`
  - `✉ Draft Follow-up`
  - `📸 Record Stock Issue` (Camera integration)

---

## 4. NOTIFICATION ERGONOMICS
AccentOS mobile uses notifications as a "Pre-Triage" layer.

- **Non-Interruptive:** Most signals go to the "Quiet Feed."
- **High-Severity Only:** Push notifications are reserved for:
  - Inventory stockouts on VIP orders.
  - MAP violations from Tier A vendors.
  - eCommerce conversion drops >50% in 1h.
- **Clear CTA:** Every notification must have a "View" and "Dismiss" action.

---

## 5. TRIAGE UX: DISMISS VS. RESOLVE
Operational speed comes from clearing the noise.

- **Swipe Left:** "Dismiss" (Hide for now, no action taken).
- **Swipe Right:** "Resolve" (Launch the workflow to fix the issue).
- **Long Press:** "Delegate" (Assign the signal to another team member).

---

## MOBILE-FIRST KPI DISPLAY
- **Outfit Font:** For labels and readability.
- **DM Mono Font:** For numeric data (ensures tabular alignment and "data-first" feel).
- **Sparklines:** Replaces large charts. Show the 7-day trend in a 100x30px space.
