# Outdoor & Architectural Lighting Cluster

## Overview

Outdoor and architectural lighting: residential exterior, landscape, commercial façade, and site lighting. Second largest revenue cluster. Longer sales cycles than decorative — typically tied to construction and renovation projects.

## Product categories

| category | typical SKUs | price range | application |
|---|---|---|---|
| Outdoor wall lanterns | 150–250 | $80–$1,500 | residential, staging |
| Post / bollard | 60–100 | $150–$800 | landscape, community |
| Pathway / step | 80–120 | $40–$300 | residential landscape |
| Flood / spot | 100–180 | $60–$500 | security, accent |
| Commercial wall packs | 40–80 | $150–$1,200 | commercial, parking |
| Canopy / soffit | 50–90 | $80–$600 | portico, covered walk |
| String / bistro | 60–100 | $30–$200 | hospitality, outdoor dining |

## Spec and compliance notes

- All outdoor fixtures must be **wet-rated or damp-rated** for the application. Flag in quote.
- Commercial projects: confirm fixture is **DLC listed** if utility rebate is in scope.
- Coastal environments: require salt-air rated finish (marine-grade). Non-coastal finishes corrode within 2 years — use as a selling point for the premium line.
- Title 24 / energy code compliance: California projects require JA8-rated LED sources on residential.

## Selling notes

- Outdoor jobs are often paired with an electrical contractor from the Trade Partners network — reference `emp-sales` SOP for co-quoting with trade partners.
- Photocell and dusk-to-dawn options nearly always desired for security fixtures — include as default in commercial quotes.
- Finish families: matte black / bronze / satin nickel are the three dominant requests. Match to interior finish family when possible (same customer, interior + exterior).

## Quoting tips

- Project quotes (landscape lighting for a full property) benefit from a site plan reference — note in quote.notes.
- Bundle pathway + spot + wall lantern as a "landscape package" to increase AOV.
- Trade margins on outdoor are narrower than decorative — check Price Book before conceding discount.

## Inventory notes

- Import lead times: 8–20 weeks for architectural/commercial spec fixtures.
- Pathway and wall lanterns move seasonally (spring + fall peak). Reorder in Jan/July to avoid stockouts.
- Commercial wall packs are large and heavy — note warehouse location for picking accuracy.
