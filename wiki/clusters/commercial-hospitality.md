# Commercial & Hospitality Lighting Cluster

## Overview

Commercial and hospitality projects: hotels, restaurants, retail buildouts, office fit-outs, and multi-family residential. Highest average deal value. Longer spec cycle — often involves lighting designer, architect, and GC in addition to the end client.

## Product categories

| category | typical SKUs | price range | application |
|---|---|---|---|
| Recessed downlights | 200–350 | $40–$400 | universal |
| Track + rail | 80–150 | $60–$600 (head) | retail, gallery, restaurant |
| Linear / cove | 100–180 | $80–$1,200/ft | hospitality, office |
| Surface cylinders | 100–160 | $80–$500 | hotel corridor, retail |
| Emergency / exit | 40–80 | $80–$400 | commercial code |
| Troffer / panel | 60–100 | $80–$600 | office |
| Decorative commercial | varies | $200–$5,000+ | lobby, restaurant |

## Spec and compliance notes

- **DLC listing** required for utility rebate eligibility on commercial LED — confirm before spec.
- **Emergency lighting** (EM) and exit signs are code-required on commercial — always quote them for new construction.
- **Title 24 / IECC** energy compliance: confirm fixture meets efficacy requirements for the jurisdiction.
- **Voltage**: commercial is typically 120V or 277V. Confirm with electrical drawings before quoting drivers.
- **AHJ approval**: some municipalities require AHJ submittal for new fixture types — note in quote.

## Selling notes

- Commercial deals run through the Sales Pipeline with longer close cycles (60–180 days typical).
- Lighting designers in the Trade Partners network often spec Accent products for their projects — maintain those relationships actively.
- Value-add: offer photometric calculations and AGI32 layout for large projects (differentiates from online competitors).
- Co-op funds from vendors can offset commercial project costs significantly — check Co-op Tracker before quoting.

## Quoting tips

- Line-item quote format is critical for commercial bids — ensure each fixture type is a separate quote line.
- Include freight estimate for large commercial orders — often $500–$2,000 for truck shipments.
- Deposit terms: commercial projects typically 50% deposit / 50% on delivery. Note in quote.notes.
- For projects with trade partners (designer / GC): use Trade Partners module to track the relationship and preferred discount structure.

## Inventory notes

- Commercial spec fixtures often drop-ship directly from vendor — flag in inventory as `import_source=vendor_direct`.
- Emergency lighting should maintain a 2-week stock buffer due to code-compliance urgency.
- Track heads and linear systems require matching accessories (feed connectors, canopies, end caps) — quote accessory kit with every system order.
