# Rubric · Lead Time

**Weight:** MEDIUM. **What it measures:** typical lead time from PO to dock for in-stock SKUs.

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **10** | Ships same/next day, in-stock items |
| **8** | 2-5 business days typical |
| **6** | 1-2 weeks typical |
| **4** | 3-5 weeks typical |
| **2** | 6-7 weeks |
| **0** | 8+ weeks consistently OR unreliable shipping |

> ⚠️ Stub — track actual lead time per vendor in `wiki/entities/vendors/<slug>.md` History section as POs ship and arrive.
