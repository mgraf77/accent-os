# Rubric · DTC / Consumer Direct

**Weight:** HIGH. **What it measures:** does the vendor sell directly to consumers in ways that undercut Accent.

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **10** | No DTC — strict dealer exclusive |
| **8** | Vendor sells direct on website but at MSRP, doesn't undercut dealers |
| **6** | Vendor has consumer site at full price, occasional promos |
| **4** | Vendor sells direct at parity or near-parity with dealer pricing |
| **2** | Vendor regularly promotes direct at margins below dealer floor |
| **0** | Vendor undercuts dealers consistently OR sells via Amazon at competitive prices |

> ⚠️ Strongly correlated with weak [[rubric-imap-enforcement]] — vendors who sell DTC are often also the channel violators they should be policing. Capture both scores together.
