# SOP: Rep Outreach

Standard process for initiating and maintaining manufacturer rep relationships.

## Outreach triggers

- New vendor onboarding (see [[sop-vendor-onboarding]])
- Rep score ([[rubric-rep-score]]) drops below 5 → escalate
- No rep contact in >90 days → re-engage
- Co-op/MDF claim pending → pursue resolution
- New promo or product line launch → request presentation
- Competitive bid requiring vendor support → request pricing/spec help

## Initial outreach (new vendor or new rep)

1. Identify rep from vendor website or L1 rep locator
2. Email introduction:
   - Introduce Accent Lighting (location, focus, volume segment)
   - Request an intro meeting (in-person if nearby, Zoom/phone if remote)
   - Ask for price list access + wholesale account setup
3. Prep for intro meeting:
   - Review vendor's top-selling SKUs and price points
   - Know current competing products Accent carries
   - Come with specific questions (see intake list below)

## Intro meeting question list

- Freight: free threshold? Drop-ship available?
- Terms: net days? Credit limit process?
- Rebates: what % at our volume? Tiered?
- MDF/co-op: % available? How do we claim?
- Display program: discount? Buyback policy? Current promos?
- IMAP: enforced? Monitoring process?
- Returns: restock fee? RGA process and timeline?
- Support: who's our dedicated rep? Territory coverage?
- L1: are you a Lighting One vendor? Lights America listing?
- Digital assets: portal access? Image/spec sheet quality?

## Ongoing rep management

- Log every interaction in AccentOS Rep Outreach module
- Update rep score quarterly (or after significant interaction)
- Flag if rep changes → re-score and re-engage
- Send quarterly performance recap: Accent's purchases, opens, pending

## Rep score escalation

Escalate rep performance issues to vendor management when score drops below 4. If rep score drops below 4:
1. Document specific failures (missed callbacks, wrong pricing, etc.)
2. Contact vendor management or regional manager directly
3. Request rep reassignment if pattern persists
4. Note escalation in AccentOS vendor notes
