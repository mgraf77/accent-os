# Rubric · Consumer Demand

**Weight:** HIGH. **What it measures:** how much organic search + brand recognition the vendor brings to the table.

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **10** | High organic search volume, strong brand recognition (Visual Comfort, Hubbardton Forge) |
| **8** | Established brand, healthy search volume |
| **6** | Recognizable in trade, moderate consumer awareness |
| **4** | Low consumer awareness, niche product |
| **2** | Trade-only brand, no consumer recognition |
| **0** | Unknown brand, no demand |
