# Lumen Output — Commercial Applications

## Footcandle targets by space type

| Space | FC (footcandles) | Notes |
|-------|-----------------|-------|
| Open office | 30–50 FC | IECC: ≤0.6 W/SF |
| Private office | 30–50 FC | Task supplement common |
| Conference room | 30–50 FC | Dimming for presentations |
| Retail general | 30–50 FC | Boost merchandise to 100+ FC |
| Retail feature display | 100–150 FC | Accent (track/spots) |
| Restaurant dining | 10–30 FC | Ambiance-driven; dim low |
| Restaurant kitchen | 50–70 FC | NSF: 50 FC min at work surfaces |
| Warehouse aisle | 20–30 FC | AGV/forklift zones higher |
| Warehouse picking | 50–75 FC | Task-critical accuracy |
| Parking garage | 3–5 FC maintained | IES RP-20-14 |
| Corridor/hallway | 10–20 FC | Emergency: 1 FC min path |
| Hotel lobby | 20–50 FC | Varies by brand standard |

## Lumen-per-watt efficiency

- Fluorescent T8 (32W 4-lamp): ~80–100 LPW
- LED troffer (40W equivalent): ~100–130 LPW
- LED high bay: 130–180 LPW (premium: 180–200+)
- Metal halide (400W): ~65–80 LPW (obsolete)
- LED retrofit: 130–160 LPW

## Calculating fixture count

```
Fixtures = (Target FC × Room SF) / (Fixture lumens × CU × LLF)
```
- **CU** (Coefficient of Utilization): 0.5–0.8 typical; from photometric data
- **LLF** (Light Loss Factor): 0.8 typical (accounts for dirt, lamp depreciation)
- **FC to lux**: 1 FC = 10.764 lux

## Common product categories at Accent

- Linear LED (troffers, strips): office, retail backroom, warehouse
- LED high bay: warehouse, distribution, gym
- Track/accent: retail merchandise display, showroom
- Downlights (4", 6"): office, hospitality, residential
- Emergency/exit combo: required in all commercial egress paths
