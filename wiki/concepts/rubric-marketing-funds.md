# Rubric · Marketing Funds

**Weight:** MEDIUM. **What it measures:** vendor-funded marketing dollars made available to Accent.

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **10** | ≥3% of purchases as marketing funds, easy to claim |
| **8** | 2-3%, easy claim process |
| **6** | 1-2%, paperwork required |
| **4** | <1% OR onerous claim process |
| **2** | Token amount, mostly unclaimable |
| **0** | No marketing funds offered |

> Compounds with [[rubric-coop]] and [[rubric-display-allowance]] — track separately to avoid double-counting.
