# Rubric: Lights America (Weight: 5)

Binary: is the vendor listed on LightsAmerica.com? This is the Lighting One buying group's consumer-facing discovery site. Listing = vendor partnership with L1, increased consumer exposure for Accent's brands.

## Scale

| Score | Condition |
|-------|-----------|
| 10 | Listed on Lights America |
| 0 | Not on Lights America |

## Notes

- LightsAmerica.com is the Lighting One member-brand consumer portal. Vendors listed there are L1-preferred or L1-approved partners.
- Scores 1–9 are unused — this is a binary gate, not a spectrum.
- Often correlated with [[rubric-l1-member]]: vendors on Lights America tend to also be L1 members.
- Key for Accent's buying group leverage: L1 membership + Lights America listing = full stack of L1 purchasing benefits.
