# Rubric: Lighting One Membership (Weight: 6)

Binary: is the vendor a Lighting One (L1) member? L1 membership unlocks negotiated rebate tiers, cooperative marketing funds, and inclusion on LightsAmerica.com.

## Scale

| Score | Condition |
|-------|-----------|
| 10 | Lighting One member |
| 0 | Not a Lighting One member |

## Notes

- Lighting One is Accent's buying group (independent lighting dealer co-op). Member vendors negotiate directly with L1 for better terms than any individual dealer could achieve alone.
- L1 members often have higher rebate programs (see [[rubric-rebates]]) specifically for L1 members.
- Scores 1–9 are unused — binary gate.
- Accent's L1 membership status determines whether these terms are accessible at all. Verify current membership is active.
- L1 membership often co-occurs with [[rubric-lights-america]] listing.
