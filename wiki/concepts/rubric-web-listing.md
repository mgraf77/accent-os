# Rubric: Web Listing (Weight: 4)

How prominently Accent Lighting appears on the vendor's dealer locator or "where to buy" page. Higher = more inbound consumer traffic routed to Accent.

## 0–10 Scale

| Score | Listing quality |
|-------|----------------|
| 10 | Featured/premier dealer (logo + top placement) |
| 9 | Prominent with map pin, link, full info |
| 8 | Listed with link + accurate contact info |
| 7 | Standard dealer locator listing |
| 6 | — |
| 5 | Listed but stale or incomplete info |
| 4 | — |
| 3 | No locator — directs to "contact your rep" |
| 2 | — |
| 1 | Authorized but not on locator |
| 0 | Not on vendor site at all |

## Notes

- Lowest weight of all categories (4) — it matters but is easily fixed with a rep call.
- Premier dealer status may require minimum annual purchase volume.
- Stale listings (wrong phone, old address, closed hours) actively hurt — verify annually.
- Some vendors only maintain locators for "preferred" dealers; authorization alone doesn't guarantee listing.
