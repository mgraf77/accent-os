# EXECUTIVE COMMAND CENTER V1 — Phase 1 Philosophy

## 1. MISSION STATEMENT
The Executive Command Center is the digital nervous system of Accent Lighting. It is not a dashboard for exploration; it is a surface for **situational awareness** and **rapid triage**. Its primary job is to protect the executive’s attention by surfacing only what is critical, while maintaining a background pulse of business health.

## 2. THE THREE-SECOND RULE
An executive should be able to look at the surface and, within three seconds, answer:
1. **Pulse:** Is the business "alive" and moving right now? (Momentum)
2. **Health:** Are the vital organs functioning within normal parameters? (Health)
3. **Friction:** Is there a specific anomaly requiring my intervention? (Signals)

## 3. CORE DESIGN PILLARS
*   **Severity-First Hierarchy:** The most dangerous signal always takes the top position, regardless of category.
*   **Rule of One:** One primary fact per card. No clusters of competing data points.
*   **Action over Observation:** Data is only surfaced if it implies a decision or a "Confirm/Dismiss" action.
*   **Mobile-Native Cognition:** Designed for the "30-second check-in" while between meetings or on the move.

## 4. OPERATIONAL DIMENSIONS (THE SIX DOTS)
The executive view is anchored by six health dimensions:
1.  **Inventory:** Stock health, dead stock, lead-time creep.
2.  **Vendor:** Performance drift, terms violations, rep responsiveness.
3.  **Fulfillment:** Shipping bottlenecks, delivery accuracy, backorder aging.
4.  **Ecommerce:** Conversion health, GMC feed status, ad efficiency.
5.  **Operational Reliability:** System uptime, data freshness, internal friction.
6.  **Stale Data Risk:** The "Confidence" score of the underlying intelligence.

## 5. EVOLUTIONARY TRAJECTORY
*   **Phase 1 (Current):** Signal-Driven Surface. Proactive surfacing of anomalies and high-leverage "Quick Wins" (Lapsed VIPs, Stagnant Quotes).
*   **Phase 2:** Agentic Partner. The system drafts the responses and executes approved workflows.
*   **Phase 3:** Autonomous OS. The digital nervous system operates silently, only alerting on system-level failures.

## 6. TRUST PRESERVATION
Trust is the most fragile component of the system.
*   **No False Alarms:** A single "False Positive" signal (e.g., alerting on a quote that was actually won but not closed in ERP) costs 10x the value of a "True Positive."
*   **Confidence Scoring:** Every signal includes a "Confidence" metric. If data is stale, the signal dims.
*   **Clear Source:** Every anomaly must be traceable back to its source (ERP, GMC, etc.) in one tap.
