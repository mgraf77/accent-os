# OPERATIONAL SURFACE HIERARCHY — ACCENTOS
> **Philosophy:** Progressive Disclosure of Complexity

AccentOS is organized into five distinct layers of operational awareness. Each layer serves a specific cognitive purpose and role-based need.

---

## 1. EXECUTIVE LAYER (The "Pulse")
*Situational Awareness for Owners & Admins*

- **Purpose:** Answer "Is the business healthy?" in 15 seconds.
- **UI Elements:** Status dots (Red/Yellow/Green), high-level trend sparklines, the "Daily Brief" card.
- **Key Metric:** Operational Momentum (Acceleration vs. Deterioration).
- **Rule of One:** Each card or dot represents exactly one primary fact.

---

## 2. OPERATOR LAYER (The "Workbench")
*Daily execution for Sales, Warehouse, and Managers*

- **Purpose:** Manage the "objects" of the business (Vendors, Customers, Orders).
- **UI Elements:** Searchable tables, filtered lists, entity "360" views.
- **Interaction:** Standard CRUD operations, relationship management, and status updates.

---

## 3. WORKFLOW LAYER (The "Orchestration")
*Transaction processing and high-value sequences*

- **Purpose:** Reduce friction in complex, multi-step tasks.
- **UI Elements:** Quote Generator, PO Receipt flows, Track Hardware Calculator, Label Batches.
- **Goal:** Minimize "clicks-to-completion" and eliminate manual data entry.

---

## 4. SIGNAL LAYER (The "Alerting")
*Exception handling and proactive monitoring*

- **Purpose:** Identify what is broken or at risk before it becomes a crisis.
- **UI Elements:** Signal Feed, Pinned Alerts, "Action Required" badges.
- **Examples:** Dead Stock, Ghost Inventory, Stagnant Quotes, Lead-time Creep.
- **UX Pattern:** Dismiss vs. Resolve.

---

## 5. INTELLIGENCE LAYER (The "Synthesis")
*Predictive insights and AI-assisted decision making*

- **Purpose:** Provide the "Why" and suggest the "Next Best Action."
- **UI Elements:** Knowledge Engine Chat, Probability Scores (Win/Loss), RFM Segment labels.
- **Agentic Path:** Drafting responses, suggesting reorder quantities, and identifying cross-sell opportunities.

---

## ROLE MAPPING TO HIERARCHY

| Role | Primary Layers | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| **Owner** | Executive, Intelligence | Hourly / Daily |
| **Manager** | Signal, Operator, Executive | Continuous |
| **Sales** | Workflow, Signal, Operator | Continuous |
| **Warehouse** | Workflow, Operator | Continuous |
