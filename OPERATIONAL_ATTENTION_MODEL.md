# OPERATIONAL ATTENTION MODEL — What Earns the Executive Glance

## 1. THE ATTENTION TAX
Every piece of data on the Executive Surface carries an **Attention Tax**. If the value of the insight does not exceed the cost of the cognitive interruption, the data must remain **Invisible**.

## 2. THE ATTENTION HIERARCHY

| Level | Filter | Disposition |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **I. CRITICAL ANOMALY** | Business survival / Revenue loss risk. | **Pulsing / Direct Alert.** |
| **II. LEVERAGE OPPORTUNITY** | High-value quick wins (e.g., Lapsed VIP). | **Top of Signal Feed.** |
| **III. DRIFT / DEGRADATION** | Performance trending away from norm. | **The "Six Dots" (Visual Pulse).** |
| **IV. ROUTINE EXECUTION** | Business as usual. | **Invisible (Module-Only).** |
| **V. NOISE / RAW DATA** | Unprocessed logs / ERP tables. | **Banned from Executive Surface.** |

## 3. WHAT SHOULD STAY INVISIBLE
The following must **NEVER** appear on the primary executive surface:
*   Individual transaction logs (unless Level 5 anomaly).
*   Full ERP-style tables with more than 3 columns.
*   "Green" status reports ("Everything is fine").
*   Speculative "AI Chat" windows without a specific prompt.
*   Incomplete data profiles (missing more than 50% of fields).

## 4. SIGNAL ADAPTATION (Self-Healing Noise Filter)
The model learns from Executive behavior:
*   **The "Ignore" Signal:** If a specific type of alert (e.g., "Freight drift < $50") is dismissed 3 times without action, the threshold for that signal is automatically raised.
*   **The "Deep Dive" Signal:** If an executive taps a specific vendor 3 times in a week, that vendor's health dot is temporarily promoted to the Executive Strip for "Close Monitoring."

## 5. TRUST PRESERVATION RULES
*   **Source Fidelity:** A signal is only as good as its data. If `inventory_value` is from a month-old CSV, the signal is downgraded to "Stale" status.
*   **No "Black Boxes":** "Probability" scores must show the contributing factors (e.g., "75% Prob - Based on: VIP status + 7d Recency").
*   **Intentional Friction:** High-impact actions (e.g., "Revoke Vendor Status") must require a double-confirm to prevent "pocket-resolution" on mobile.

## 6. EXECUTIVE OUTCOMES
Success is measured by:
1.  **Reduction in "Surprise":** "I knew about that lead-time creep before the rep called me."
2.  **Increased Triage Speed:** "I cleared the business friction in 30 seconds while waiting for coffee."
3.  **Strategic Focus:** "I didn't spend my morning looking for problems; the system brought the problems to me."
