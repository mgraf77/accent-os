# EXECUTIVE SIGNAL SUMMARY PATTERNS
> **Persona:** The Owner (Michael, Paul, Patrick)
> **Constraint:** Mobile-first, 3-second "Glanceability"

## 1. THE EXECUTIVE STRIP (STATUS DOTS)
A persistent row of 6 "Pulse" dots at the very top of the mobile view or in the command center rail.

- **The Pattern:** Six dots, one for each Risk Dimension (Inventory, Vendor, Fulfillment, Ecommerce, Operational, Stale Data).
- **Behavior:**
  - 🟢 Green: No active warnings.
  - 🟡 Yellow: Active warning(s) in this dimension.
  - 🔴 Red: Level 5 Critical alert active.
- **Interaction:** Tap a dot → Modal summary of that dimension only.

## 2. COMMAND-CENTER SUMMARIES (THE "BIG THREE")
Executives should not see the full queue. They see the "Aggregated Pulse."

- **Pattern:**
  1. **Business Momentum:** (Revenue YTD vs. Target | Pipeline Growth WoW).
  2. **Operational Health:** (Overdue PO Value | Stale Quote Liability).
  3. **Risk Heatmap:** (Dimensional severity sum).

## 3. SEVERITY HEATMAPS
A visual grid (6x6 or 3x3) showing severity across business modules.

- **Executive View:** High-level blocks of color.
- **Example:**
  - [ Sales: 🟢 ] [ Warehouse: 🟡 ] [ Vendor: 🔴 ]
  - [ Ecom: 🟢 ] [ Admin: 🟢 ] [ Data: 🟡 ]
- **Value:** Instant identification of which "department" is struggling today.

## 4. KPI OVERLAYS
Signals shouldn't live in a silo; they should be "overlaid" on top of KPIs.

- **Pattern:** When looking at the "Revenue YTD" KPI, show a small "Risk Badge."
- **Example:** "$18,541 Revenue ⚠️ *($42K at risk from stale quotes)*"

## 5. OPERATIONAL DIGEST BEHAVIOR
The executive shouldn't have to "hunt" for information. The system "presents" it.

- **Morning Digest:** A 3-bullet summary of the "State of the Union" generated by the Decision Engine.
- **Evening Close:** A summary of "Signal Debt" paid down today vs. new signals generated.

## 6. THE "RULE OF ONE" ON MOBILE
Every card/tile shown to an executive on mobile must have **one primary fact.**

- **Incorrect:** Card with 5 different stats.
- **Correct:** "90% of your revenue is coming from Tier A vendors. (No change)"
- **Correct:** "You have $12,400 in co-op funds expiring this month. [ View List ]"
