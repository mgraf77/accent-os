# EXECUTIVE QUEUE — SCAN PATTERNS
> **Objective:** Cognitive speed. High-level situational awareness.

## 1. THE EXECUTIVE STRIP (HUD)
A 6-dot status bar at the top of every executive screen.

```text
+-----------------------------------------------------------+
| [●] [●] [●] [●] [●] [○]                                   |
| INV VEN FUL ECO OPS STA                                   |
+-----------------------------------------------------------+
  ^ Dot size: 12px | Spacing: 16px | Labels: Outfit 8px
```

*   **INV:** Inventory (Stockouts)
*   **VEN:** Vendor (Lead Time)
*   **FUL:** Fulfillment (Deliveries)
*   **ECO:** Ecommerce (Conversion)
*   **OPS:** Operational (Confidence/Rot)
*   **STA:** Stale Data (Sync Status)

---

## 2. OCCULAR ANCHORS (SCAN PATH)
Executives scan for **Exceptions**, not confirmations. The layout is optimized for an "F-Pattern" scan.

### Anchor 1: The Status Dot (Top Left)
The eye starts at the Executive Strip. Any non-green dot triggers a "Drill-Down" intent.

### Anchor 2: The Primary Momentum (Center)
A large, centered metric in `DM Mono` (e.g., `+14% Acceleration`). This provides immediate emotional context for the day's performance.

### Anchor 3: The Exception List (Bottom)
Only the top 3 "High Leverage" signals are shown. No scrolling is required for initial awareness.

---

## 3. COLOR AS SEVERITY
Executives only care about **Acceleration** and **Deterioration**.

*   **Red `#ed1c24`:** Significant Deterioration (Action Required).
*   **Green `#10b981`:** Significant Acceleration (Validation/Reward).
*   **Grey `#9ca3af`:** Stable/Normal (Ignore).

---

## 4. TYPOGRAPHY AS HIERARCHY
Speed is achieved by separating "What it is" from "How it's doing."

*   **"What it is":** `Outfit` (Sans-serif) for labels and category names.
*   **"How it's doing":** `DM Mono` (Monospace) for all percentages, dollar amounts, and counts.

---

## 5. THE "ONE THING" SUMMARY
Every Executive Screen includes a natural language summary at the top (generated by heuristics):
> "Inventory is stable, but **Minka Group** lead times are deteriorating. 3 Customer orders at risk."
